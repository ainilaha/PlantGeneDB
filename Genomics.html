1.1 Genomics

Elymus nutans
is a perennial herb of the gramineae family, mainly distributed in the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau at an altitude of 3000-4500 m. As a high quality forage, it has the characteristics of cold resistance and drought resistance and high nutritional value. The genome size is 4.5Gb and the number of chromosomes is 2n=42.
